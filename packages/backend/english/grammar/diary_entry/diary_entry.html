ማስታወሻ ደብተር መጻፍDiary entry?

Definition

A diary entry is a record and reflection on personal experience. The number of entries will be determined by the question.

A diary entry

  • must ያንፀባርቁ reflect a date for each entry
  • must be written in the first person
  • must express feeling and emotions
  • will be መደበኛ ያልሆነ informal in style

Example of diary entry

27 APRIL 2018
Dear Diary
My nerves are ተሰባበረ shattered . My stomach is filled with butterflies and my hands are sweaty and won’t stop እየተንቀጠቀጠ shaking . I’m so nervous about this talent show tonight. What on earth made me decide to compete in the first place and why did I choose singing as my talent?
