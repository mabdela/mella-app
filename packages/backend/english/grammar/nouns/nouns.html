What is a ስሞችNoun?

Words that refer to a ሰው person , an እንስሳ animal , a ቦታ place , a ነገር thing , an ሀሳብ idea , a ስሜት feeling or ግዛት state are called ስሞች nouns .

Examples

  • Common Nouns - አውሮፕላን Aircraft , መገንባት Building , ሮዝ Rose , ጠርሙስ Bottle , ሠዓሊ Painter , ተማሪ Student .
  • Proper Nouns - እንግሊዝኛ English , ሰኞ Monday , ሙምባይ Mumbai , የሰው ስም Godavari , የሰው ስም Mahesh .
  • Abstract Nouns - ሀብት Wealth , ጀግንነት Bravery , ድምጽ Voice , ደግነት Kindness , እውነት Truth .
  • Collective Nouns - ታዳሚዎች Audience , ቤተሰብ Family , ጥቅል Bunch , መንጋ Flock , እቅፍ አበባ Bouquet .

Singular and Plural Possessive Nouns

In English grammar, an apostrophe is used to indicate ownership. However, apostrophes can be tricky. This article is intended to help you understand how to use apostrophes correctly with singular & plural nouns.

Singular possessive nouns are easy to form. If a person, place, or thing owns something, you just need to add 's.

  • Our boss’s car is pretty ውድ expensive .
  • The የምግብ አዘገጃጀት መመሪያዎች recipe’s ንጥረ ነገሮች ingredients are not easy to find.

Plural possessive nouns are formed by adding s' to the end of the singular word.

  • The kids’ toys are ተበታተነ scattered all over the room.
  • Most of the books’ pages were ተጎድቷል damaged in the library fire.

However, you should note that if a plural noun does not end in an s plural, you should simply add 's to the end of the noun to create its plural form.

  • The women’s ጌጣጌጥ jewellery was pretty expensive.
  • Electrical የአይጦች mice’s traps are more effective than the traditional ones.
