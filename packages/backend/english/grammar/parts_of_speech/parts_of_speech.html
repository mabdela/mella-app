የንግግር ክፍሎች Parts of speech

All words may be classified into groups called parts of speech.

There are 8 parts of speech namely:

  1. ስሞች Nouns

  2. ግሶች verbs

  3. ተውላጠ ስም pronouns

  4. ቅፅሎች adjectives

  5. ተዉሳካ ግስ adverbs

  6. ማያያዣዎች conjunctions

  7. ማያያዣዎች interjections

  8. ቅድመ -ግምቶች prepositions
