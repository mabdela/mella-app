The ቅድመ -ግምቶችPrepositions

A preposition is a word that shows the ግንኙነት relationship between other words in a sentence.

Example

The cat ተኛ lay under the table.

Hence a preposition is a word that links another word or word group to the rest of the sentence. The noun or pronoun after the preposition is called the object of the preposition.

The table is the object of the preposition under in the above sentence.

The preposition under relates the verb lay to the noun table.
