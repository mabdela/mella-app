The ሥርዓተ ነጥብPunctuation

Punctuation is the system of symbols that we use to separate sentences and parts of sentences, and to make their meaning clear.

Punctuation marks can be grouped into:

  1. End marks
    • The full stop(.)
    • The question mark(?)
    • The exclamation mark(!)
  2. The comma(,)
  3. The semicolon(;)
  4. The semicolon(;) and colon(:)
  5. The hyphen(-)
  6. The apostoph(')
  7. The quotaion mark("")
